    • My Love for Finance

    22 AUGUST 2021

    Recently during lockdown, when we were at home I got intrested in Finance and I started reading about Why it is important for every individial to understand Personal Finance. I came to know that people who dont understand Personal Finance and its importance, they are observed to make bad financial decisions in life. This may not show its effect instantly but they will see its effect in the near future.

    Now you may say, what are bad decisions?

  • 1. Buying an Iphone 12 series on EMI
  • 2. Not saving money as an emergency fund.
  • 3. Buying a new car, that too on EMI. etc...

    • I'm not saying that you should not be buying these things at all. But what I'm saying here is that be aware and mindfull of your Financial Decicions.

    That's all I had to share with you for today.
    Until next time, I would like you to read about Personal Finance and be mindfull of your financial decisions
